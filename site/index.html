faq

is shareclick free?

yes — completely free and open source (MIT / Apache-2.0). no account, no subscription, no cloud.

does it share a mouse & keyboard between Mac and Windows?

yes. shareclick is a software KVM: one keyboard and mouse control both your Mac and Windows PC over the local network. move the cursor to a screen edge to switch machines.

is it a free alternative to Synergy and ShareMouse?

yes. it does what paid tools like Synergy and ShareMouse do — input sharing, clipboard sync and file transfer — but free, open source, and built for lower input lag.

does it sync the clipboard and transfer files?

yes. the clipboard (text and images) syncs automatically, and you can send files straight across the encrypted connection.

is it secure? does it need the internet?

LAN-only, no cloud. every channel is end-to-end encrypted with X25519 + ChaCha20-Poly1305, authenticated by a shared passphrase.
